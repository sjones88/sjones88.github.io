About Steve:

Steve Jones is an aspiring software developer and is currently a student at Dev Bootcamp Chicago. He is a proud native Chicagoan.

Steve holds a BA in philosophy and biology from Lawrence University; he has diverse work and entrepreneurial experience, having worked as a lab tech in biomedical research, in commercial real estate brokerage, and at an organic waste recycling startup.

Steve loves to learn and create, and he is excited about building beautiful things with code.
